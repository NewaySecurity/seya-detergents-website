Our Story

Seya Detergents is a 100% Black-owned, proudly South African brand delivering high-quality, eco-conscious cleaning products to homes, resellers, and businesses nationwide. Our goal is simple: make cleaning more powerful, affordable, and sustainable for everyone.

Founded and headquartered in Greenvalley, Bushbuckridge (Mapulaneng), our brand draws strength from local community values and a deep connection to the land. We believe in producing solutions that not only work hard but also honor people and planet.
